## Description

Inkling turns messy meetings into clean, useful notes — without sending a single
word to the cloud.

Record a conversation and watch it transcribe live, on your device. Jot your own
notes at the same time — type them, write with Apple Pencil on iPad, or snap a
photo of your paper notebook and Inkling reads the handwriting. When you're done,
Inkling merges everything into a clean wrap‑up: a summary, decisions, action
items, and open questions you can edit and keep.

It all runs on device. No third‑party servers, no account, no tracking. Your
notes sync across your own iPhone, iPad, and Mac through your private iCloud.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Live, on‑device transcription of your meetings
• Take notes your way — typed (with bold, italic, headers, bullets), Apple
  Pencil, or a photo of handwritten pages (read on device)
• AI wrap‑up that merges your notes + transcript into a clean, editable summary
  with action items — adapts to a quick note or a multi‑person meeting
• Push action items to Reminders; create calendar events from a note
• A built‑in calendar (month / week / day) with on‑device meeting prep
• Ask Inkling: a private assistant that answers questions across your notes
• Search everything — notes, transcripts, wrap‑ups, even text in your photos
• Syncs across your devices via your private iCloud

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything happens on your device. Inkling collects no data, shows no ads, and
has no accounts. The AI is Apple's on‑device intelligence — nothing is uploaded.

Requires iOS/iPadOS/macOS 26. On‑device AI features need a device that supports
Apple Intelligence.
